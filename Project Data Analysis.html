What is Anomaly Detection?

Anomaly detection is the process of identifying unexpected items or events in data, which do not conform to an expected pattern. This technique is used in various fields such as fraud detection, network security, and medical diagnosis.

Types of Anomaly Detection

  • Supervised Anomaly Detection: Involves labeled data to train the model to detect anomalies.
  • Unsupervised Anomaly Detection: Does not require labeled data and works by identifying outliers based on statistical properties.
  • Semi-supervised Anomaly Detection: Combines both labeled and unlabeled data to detect anomalies.

Why Quantum AI?

Quantum computing can process complex datasets faster than classical computers. By using quantum AI, we can achieve more efficient anomaly detection, especially with large and high-dimensional datasets.
